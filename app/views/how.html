How to use

After doing any assessment, if you want to start again with a different service, then you need to clear the data first.

Ethics assessment

There are 8 screens to go through, you will:

  • create some examples of potential harms your product or service might cause
  • have next steps for them
  • give a priority to those you need to take action on

As always, the value is not so much in the results but more in the discussion within your team generated by the questions.

Scottish Government Service standard

There are 17 screens to go through, you will:

  • assess each criteria under each of the 14 points
  • have a summary of where you are for each of them
