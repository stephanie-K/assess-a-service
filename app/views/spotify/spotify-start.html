Why should you do an Ethics assessment?

The Spotify.Design worksheet aims to help you evaluate your products or services for any potential harms and prioritise the next steps.

  • helps you anticipate potential intended and unintended consequences
  • created from the user's perspective as they are the ones potentially impacted
  • tool to start a conversation and investigation, not and 'end-all' approach

How it works

List of potential negative effects

They are broken down into 3 categories of potential harms: physical, emotional and societal

Examples of how you might encourage or cause this effect

For each effect, you will list examples you might think of.

Look beyond the user you're designing for: it could impact those who don’t use your product or service as well

Chance of effect - Scale from 1 to 5

A 5-point scale describes the degree of confidence your team has that your product or service produces a certain effect.

{% include "../includes/chance-of-effect-scale.html" %}

Level of concern - Scale from 1 to 5

A 5-point scale describes the level of concern your team has about this particular harm, where 5 = Greatest concern and 1 = Least concern.
