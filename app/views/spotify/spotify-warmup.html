Ethics assessment - Warm-up

Before jumping into this assessment, you might want to do a little warm up using one or more of the below questions, borrowed from Artefact Group’s Tarot Cards of Tech:

  • How could a bad actor use this product? What is your team doing to prepare for that?
  • What about your product model would concern the people using it the most?
  • What’s the worst headline about your product you can imagine?
  • How might a community change if 80% of residents used your product?
